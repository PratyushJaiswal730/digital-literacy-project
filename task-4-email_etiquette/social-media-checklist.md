## ✅ Do’s
- Think before you post — ensure your content adds value  
- Be kind, inclusive, and respectful to all users  
- Double-check facts and sources before sharing information  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- Maintain a healthy balance between online and offline life  
- Customize privacy settings to control who sees your content  
- Report inappropriate or harmful behavior when you see it  

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t overshare personal details like location or daily routines  
- Avoid reacting emotionally or posting in anger  
- Don’t trust or forward suspicious messages or links  
- Avoid comparing yourself negatively with others online  
- Don’t engage with trolls or encourage negative behavior  
- Avoid using social media excessively or addictively  
- Don’t ignore platform guidelines and community standards  

## 🌐 Stay Smart Online
Your online actions shape your reputation — use social media wisely and responsibly.
